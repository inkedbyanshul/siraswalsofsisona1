Are donations tax exempted?

Donations to charitable organizations and trusts are tax deductible under Section 80G. Section 80GGB allows registered Indian firms to claim tax deductions for donations to political parties.

Who are the beneficiaries of the scholarship?

Students pursuing Bachelor degrees, carefully selected through a NGO devised process to select deserving students

How do you make the fee payment for students?

We usually transfer funds to students directly to students' institutions through draft or through ways suitable for the student and institutions
